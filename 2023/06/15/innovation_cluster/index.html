基于差异化创新模式的区域创新集群支撑要素优化工具Regional Innovation Cluster Support Factor Optimization Tool Based on Differentiated Innovation Patterns

2023-06-15
2023-06-16
DBSCAN, GBDT, Hierarchical Clustering

为了实现创新集群的空间、模式精准识别和要素需求、发展趋势精准判定，该研究设计分为创新集群空间识别、创新集群模式分类、创新支撑要素相关分析、要素投放效益情景模拟4个步骤，紧密结合DBSCAN、梯度提升决策树等机器学习技术实现基于大量专利数据的创新集群发展模型支持。

In order to achieve accurate spatial and pattern identification of innovation clusters and accurate determination of factor demand and development trend, the research design is divided into four steps: spatial identification of innovation clusters, pattern classification of innovation clusters, correlation analysis of innovation support factors, and simulation of factor placement benefit scenarios, closely combined with machine learning technologies such as DBSCAN and gradient boosting decision tree to realize model support for innovation cluster development based on a large amount of patent data.

（1）创新集群空间识别：主要通过专利申请数据识别创新活动聚集发生的创新集群。首先对专利数据库中的2020年数据进行数据清洗，通过专利申请人地址信息进行地理编码获得其经纬度坐标实现空间落位，并基于专利数据中的共同合作属性获取专利联系维度信息；随后应用基于密度的机器学习聚类方法DBSCAN对专利点数据进行创新集群识别，调整邻域参数获取合适的集群面数据；最后应用Tin工具中的外包凹多边形创建进行集群形态优化，获得最终集群空间范围。该步骤中使用的DBSCAN技术有效地应对了传统划定方法形状复杂、缺少预定簇的问题，提升了识别结果的空间精度。

(1) Spatial identification of innovation clusters: mainly through the patent application data to identify the innovation clusters where innovation activities gather and occur. Firstly, we clean the 2020 data in the patent database, geocode the address information of patent applicants to obtain their latitude and longitude coordinates to achieve spatial location, and obtain patent linkage dimension information based on the common cooperation attributes in the patent data; then we apply the density-based machine learning clustering method DBSCAN to identify innovation clusters on the patent point data, adjust the neighborhood parameters to obtain suitable Finally, we apply the outsourced concave polygon creation in the Tin tool to optimize the cluster shape and obtain the final cluster spatial extent. The DBSCAN technique used in this step effectively copes with the problems of complex shapes and lack of predefined clusters in the traditional delineation method, and improves the spatial accuracy of the identification results.

（2）创新集群模式分类：主要依托创新活动自身类别、主体、联系等属性分析创新集群的模式特征。首先通过点-群属性的连接统计将专利数据的属性再集群层级加以反映，主要以内部比例结构的方式体现其创新活动的类型、主体，同时应用SNA（社会网络分析）、ANN（平均最近临）等算法，通过Python处理其创新联系特征和集聚特征。其次通过方法比选，选择机器学习中的层次聚类方法完成分类，并输出聚类属性在不同簇中的贡献度表作为进行模式特征比较依据。该步骤面对数据规模大，难以通过GIS平台进行空间统计的问题，使用Python中的Geopandas等地理处理库进行数据处理，同时使用了不需预设簇数的层次聚类方法应对确定模式类别的主观性。

(2) Innovation cluster pattern classification: The pattern characteristics of innovation clusters are mainly analyzed by relying on the attributes of innovation activities themselves, such as types, subjects and connections. Firstly, the attributes of patent data are reflected at the cluster level through the connection statistics of point-cluster attributes, and the types and subjects of their innovation activities are mainly reflected in the way of internal proportional structure, while SNA (Social Network Analysis) and ANN (Average Nearest Pro) algorithms are applied to process their innovation linkage characteristics and clustering characteristics through Python. Secondly, through method comparison, the hierarchical clustering method in machine learning is selected to complete the classification, and the contribution degree table of clustering attributes in different clusters is output as the basis for conducting pattern feature comparison. This step confronts the problem of large data scale and the difficulty of spatial statistics through GIS platform, and uses geoprocessing libraries such as Geopandas in Python for data processing, and also uses a hierarchical clustering method that does not require a predetermined number of clusters to cope with the subjectivity of determining pattern categories.

（3）创新支撑要素相关分析：主要基于模式识别结果进行创新支撑要素的相对重要性和影响权重分析。首先应用Python对链家、百度地图等平台的空间属性数据进行抓取，结合栅格GDP数据、创新支持性政策等多源数据完善支撑要素框架。其次，应用梯度提升决策树（GBDT）分析不同组别创新集群的支撑要素相对重要性，并结合多元线性方法对照分析。最终判定不同类别创新集群的重点支撑要素。由于一般的相关回归方法导出p value无法评判属性之间的相对重要性，本步中选择的GBDT模型输出结果实现了有效补充。

(3) Correlation analysis of innovation support elements: The relative importance and influence weight analysis of innovation support elements are mainly based on the pattern identification results. Firstly, Python is applied to capture the spatial attribute data from platforms such as Chain Home and Baidu Map, combined with the raster GDP data, innovation supporting policies and other multi-source data to improve the framework of support elements. Secondly, Gradient Boosted Decision Tree (GBDT) is applied to analyze the relative importance of the support elements of different groups of innovation clusters, and combined with multivariate linear method to control the analysis. Finally, the key supporting elements of different categories of innovation clusters are determined. Since the general correlation regression method to derive p value cannot judge the relative importance among attributes, the output of GBDT model selected in this step achieves an effective complement.

（4）要素投放效益情景模拟：基于2015-2020年历年专利数据，结合要素相关分析中的权重结果进行趋势外推，探讨在不同要素供给模式下创新集群的发展效果。使用神经网络进行情景推演，并完成支撑要素供需优化平台的前端设计。

(4) Scenario simulation of factor placement benefits: Based on the patent data for the calendar years 2015-2020, the trend extrapolation is carried out by combining the weighting results in factor-related analysis to explore the development effects of innovation clusters under different factor supply modes. The scenario extrapolation is conducted using neural networks and the front-end design of the supporting factor supply and demand optimization platform is completed.
