县的城镇化的特征、效能及短板识别：以河南省82县为例 The characteristics, efficiency and short board identification of county urbanization:Taking 82 counties in Henan Province as examples

2023-02-26
2023-02-26
Writing


县的城镇化是当前我国新型城镇化战略实施的重要层级，对于改善城乡关系、促进经济社会和谐可持续发展有着关键意义。县与农业紧密相连，但现有的研究很少能够脱开县级市讲述出专属于县的独特故事。本文选择河南省的县作为研究对象，以田野调查结合定量分析，归纳县的城镇化特征，评析县城的城镇化效能与短板，研判县的城镇化趋势并尝试给出提出应对策略。县的人口、产业、生计生活、城镇体系、城镇建设、公共服务在过去20年经历了全面转型， 在人口、经济、行政、农业安全、生态和社会方面发挥重要了作用。研究认为，未来县将继续稳步实现传统农业社会向现代社会的演进，表现为产业经济、人口结构、城乡关系的全面、综合性转变，应当从产业、公共服务和制度建设层面对这些新趋势加以应对。

The urbanization of counties is an important level in the implementation of my country’s new urbanization strategy, which is of key significance for improving urban-rural relations and promoting harmonious and sustainable economic and social development. Counties are closely related to agriculture, but few existing studies can tell unique stories of counties apart from county-level cities. This paper selects counties in Henan Province as the research object, combines field surveys with quantitative analysis, summarizes the urbanization characteristics of counties, evaluates the urbanization efficiency and shortcomings of counties, studies and judges the urbanization trend of counties, and tries to propose countermeasures. The county’s population, industry, livelihood, urban system, urban construction, and public services have undergone a comprehensive transformation in the past 20 years, and have played an important role in population, economy, administration, agricultural security, ecology, and society. The study believes that in the future, the county will continue to steadily realize the evolution from traditional agricultural society to modern society, manifested as a comprehensive and comprehensive transformation of industrial economy, population structure, and urban-rural relations. These new trends should be considered from the aspects of industry, public services, and system construction response.
